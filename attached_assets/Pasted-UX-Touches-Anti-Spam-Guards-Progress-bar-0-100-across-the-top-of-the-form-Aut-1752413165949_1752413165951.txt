Post‑Submit Flow
    1. Success toast: “Thanks! Your post is in review and will appear soon.”
    2. Redirect to /dashboard/submissions with status Pending Review.
    3. Admin review toggles Live status; once approved, the post card:
        ◦ Displays tags as clickable pills → /search?tag=<tagSlug>
        ◦ Shows Blogger Display Name; clicking opens the blogger’s profile.
